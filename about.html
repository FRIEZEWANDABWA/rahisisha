About Rahisisha Tech

We're a forward-thinking technology company dedicated to simplifying business operations through innovative AI solutions and cutting-edge development practices across Africa.

Our Story

Founded with a vision to democratize AI technology across Africa, Rahisisha Tech emerged from the recognition that businesses needed accessible, practical AI solutions to compete in the digital age.

2024

Foundation

Founded in 2024 with a vision to democratize AI technology across Africa, starting with a passionate team in Nairobi, Kenya.

Q2 2024

First Client Success

Delivered our first AI automation solution, helping a local business reduce processing time by 60% and achieve significant cost savings.

Q3 2024

Service Expansion

Expanded our offerings to include comprehensive web development, mobile apps, and digital marketing services.

Q4 2024

Growth Milestone

Reached 125+ completed projects and launched our AI training programs to empower businesses with practical AI skills.

Our Mission

To democratize AI technology and make it accessible to businesses of all sizes across Africa, enabling them to compete globally while solving local challenges through innovative technology solutions.

Our Vision

A Message from Our Founder

"When we founded Rahisisha Tech in 2024, we had a simple but powerful vision: to make AI technology accessible and practical for African businesses. We've seen firsthand how the right technology can transform operations, increase efficiency, and drive growth.

Our name 'Rahisisha' means 'to make easier' in Swahili, and that's exactly what we do. We take complex AI and technology concepts and make them simple, practical, and valuable for our clients.

Today, I'm proud to say we've helped over 125 businesses across Africa leverage technology to achieve their goals. But we're just getting started. The future of business in Africa is digital, and we're here to make that transition as smooth and successful as possible."

Frieze Wandabwa
